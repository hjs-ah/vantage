Frequently asked questions

How do I know which lab is right for me? +

If you're job searching or angling for a promotion, start with Lab 01. If you coach or develop others professionally, Lab 02. If you're building something new, Lab 03. Quick book and we'll confirm in the intake.

Can I combine labs? +

Yes. A common pairing is Vision Clarity for the business and Coaching Stack for the team being built around it. Reach out and we'll scope accordingly.

What is the agentic AI assistant? +

It's an AI assistant trained on your specific coaching materials and frameworks — so your clients get consistent, on-brand support between sessions. It's the same infrastructure behind our Chess Not Checkers mentorship program, now productized for other coaches.

Do you work with teams, not just individuals? +

Yes. Lab 02 and Lab 03 are both designed to scale from a solo practitioner to a small team. Multi-coach licensing is coming in Phase 2.
